Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Melodia?

Melodia is an AI-powered music generator that allows users to create and download custom music tracks. You can select the number of notes and instruments, and generate your own unique song.

2. How does Melodia generate music?

Melodia uses a machine learning model trained on a large set of musical data to generate sequences of notes. These sequences are then converted into MIDI files, which can be downloaded and played using your preferred instrument.

3. Can I select the instrument for my music?

Yes! You can choose from a variety of instruments, such as piano, guitar, and violin, when generating your music.

4. How long does it take to generate music?

The music generation process usually takes a few seconds, depending on the number of notes and other settings. You will be notified once your music is ready to download.

5. Can I download the music generated by Melodia?

Yes! After generating the music, you can download it in MP3 formats.
