Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Melodia?

Melodia is an AI-powered music generator that allows users to create and download custom music tracks. You can select the number of notes and instruments, speed of song and generate your own unique song.

2. How does Melodia generate music?

Melodia uses a machine learning model trained on a large set of musical data, specifically utilizing an LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) Neural Network to generate sequences of musical notes. These sequences are then converted into MIDI files, which can be downloaded and played using your preferred instrument. Notably, Melodia’s dataset includes music from the popular Final Fantasy series, ensuring high-quality music generation inspired by iconic video game soundtracks.

What is Melodia suitable for?
Melodia is particularly suitable for creating custom music for various purposes, such as video games, background music for videos, personal projects, or even experimenting with new musical ideas. Its flexibility in allowing users to choose the number of notes, instruments, and speed makes it ideal for generating unique music tailored to specific needs.

3. Can I select the instrument for my music?

Yes! You can choose from a variety of instruments, such as piano, guitar, and violin, when generating your music.

4. How long does it take to generate music?

The music generation process usually takes a few seconds, depending on the number of notes and other settings. You will be notified once your music is ready to download.

5. Can I download the music generated by Melodia?

Yes! After generating the music, you can download it in MP3 formats.

6. How do I choose the number of notes for my music?

When generating your custom music on Melodia, you can specify the number of musical notes you want to include in your track. This allows you to control the complexity and length of the generated song. Here’s how you can choose the number of notes:

  • Simple Tunes (Fewer Notes): If you want a shorter or simpler tune, you can select around 50-100 notes. This is ideal for quick background music or simple melodies.
  • Complex Tunes (More Notes): For more elaborate and longer pieces, you can select 200 or more notes. This is useful for creating richer and more intricate music.

How to select: Before generating your music, look for the input field or slider labeled "Number of Notes" on the music creation page. Simply adjust the value to the desired number of notes you want in your song.

Note: The higher the number of notes, the longer it may take for the music generation process, so feel free to experiment to find the right balance for your needs.
